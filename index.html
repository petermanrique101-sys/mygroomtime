For Groomers

Run your shop, not your inbox.

Stop juggling spreadsheets, paper invoices, and missed texts. MyGroomTime handles the busywork so you can focus on the dogs.

  • ✓Online booking — clients self-book, you approve. Calendar stays clean.
  • ✓Built-in billing — send invoices, take cards, get paid same day.
  • ✓Pet profiles — temperament notes, allergies, last cut, photos, vaccine records.
  • ✓Auto-reminders — text + email for upcoming appointments and rebooks.
  • ✓Customer photo uploads — owners send the cut they want. No more guessing.
For Pet Parents

Your dog's grooming, simplified.

Book in seconds, see your pup's full history, and never dig through a glovebox for vaccine paperwork again.

  • ✓Find local groomers with real reviews and real photos.
  • ✓Book instantly — pick the date, pick the service, done.
  • ✓Upload reference photos so your groomer nails the cut every time.
  • ✓Vaccine + shot records stored once, shared with any new groomer.
  • ✓Pay & tip in-app — no awkward checkout fumbling.
